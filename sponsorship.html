We are capable of rasing $17,000 annually for our Post Prom Senior Event, and that is largely in part to our generous sponsors. Your tax-deductible gift in support of this event is greatly appreciated and will be recognized in our Fashion Show program.


There are different levels of sponsorship and ALL levels are so greatly appreciated! If you donate $250.00 or more, you will become a Platinum Sponsor. If you donate between $200.00 and $250.00, you will become a Gold Sponsor. If you donate between $100.00 and $200.00, you will become a Silver Sponsor. If you donate between $50.00 and $100.00, you will become a Bronze Sponsor. All levels of Sponsors will be recongnized in the program on the night of the fashion show.


Sponsorship checks may be made payable to Notre Dame High School Senior Fashion Show. Should you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me directly. Thank you for your anticipated support and generosity!
